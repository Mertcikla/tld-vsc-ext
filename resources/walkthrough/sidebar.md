## Diagrams and Element Library

Click the **tlDiagram** icon in the activity bar to access two native panels:

- **Diagrams** — browse, open, rename, and delete diagrams
- **Element Library** — pick reusable elements and drop them into any open diagram

[Show Diagrams](command:tldiagram.focusDiagramsView) [Show Element Library](command:tldiagram.focusElementLibraryView)
